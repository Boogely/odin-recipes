Description

Sincs or "Sincronizadas" are a very simple and traditional Mexican dish comprised of corn tortillas, ham, and cheese. While the cheese choice is totally up to the individual (i.e some people use queso de oaxaca or mozzeralla) this recipe will use muenster.

Ingredients

The recipe consists of the following

  • Ham
  • Cheese (Preferably Meunster)
  • Mexican Corn Tortillas
  • Cooking Oil

Steps

  1. Heat a large flat plan on medium high
  2. While pan heats, put together the sincs using 2 tortillas, 1 slice of ham and cheese
  3. When the pan is hot, add some cooking oil
  4. Place your sincs in any open space on the pan and let it cook for 2 minutes
  5. Flip the tortilla and let it cook for another 2 minutes
  6. Remove from pan and serve with condiments (ketchup, hot sauce, tomatillo sauce, etc.)
