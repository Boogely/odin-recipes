Sincs

Sincs or "Sincronizadas" are a very simple and traditional Mexican dish comprised of corn tortillas, ham, and cheese. While the cheese choice is totally up to the individual (i.e some people use queso de oaxaca or mozzeralla) this recipe will use muenster.

Ingredients

The recipe consists of the following

    Ham
    Cheese (Preferably Meunster)
    Mexican Corn Tortillas
    Cooking Oil

Steps

    Heat a large flat plan on medium high
    While pan heats, put together the sincs using 2 tortillas, 1 slice of ham and cheese
    When the pan is hot, add some cooking oil
    Place your sincs in any open space on the pan and let it cook for 2 minutes
    Flip the tortilla and let it cook for another 2 minutes
    Remove from pan and serve with condiments (ketchup, hot sauce, tomatillo sauce, etc.)
